## Get Started in Four Steps

Follow this onboarding flow to go from complete beginner to certified AI learner.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Free Account">
    Sign up at AI Academy in under two minutes. All you need is an email address to get started — no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Learning Path">
    Select the path that matches your goals. If you're brand new to AI, the Beginner Path is the perfect place to start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a Course">
    Dive into bite-sized video lessons, interactive quizzes, and hands-on projects — all designed to build your skills progressively.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Your Certificate">
    Complete your learning path and receive a shareable certificate you can add to your resume or LinkedIn profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **No experience? No problem.** AI Academy is built specifically for beginners. Every course starts from the ground up, so you don't need a background in math, coding, or technology to succeed here.
</Tip>

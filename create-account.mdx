Creating your AI Academy account takes less than two minutes and gives you immediate access to introductory lessons, learning path previews, and the learner community. All you need to get started is a valid email address — no payment details or prior experience required. Follow the steps below to set up your account and begin your first lesson today.

## Sign Up for AI Academy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page">
    Open AI Academy in your browser and click the **Sign Up** button on the home page. You can register with your email address or use your existing Google or LinkedIn account for a faster sign-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Provide your name, email address, and a secure password. If you're signing up with Google or LinkedIn, these details are filled in automatically. Read and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from AI Academy. Open the email and click **Confirm My Email**. If you don't see it within a few minutes, check your spam folder or request a new verification email from the sign-up page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your First Learning Path">
    Once your email is verified, you'll be guided through a short onboarding questionnaire. Answer a few questions about your background and goals, and AI Academy will recommend the best learning path for you. Select a path to add it to your dashboard — you can always change it later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Your First Course">
    Open your chosen learning path and click **Start Course** on the first course in the sequence. Your first lesson will begin immediately. Welcome to AI Academy — you're on your way.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Account

After signing up, take a few moments to configure your account so your learning experience works exactly the way you want it.

- **Profile.** Add a profile photo and a short bio to your learner profile. A complete profile helps you connect with other learners in the community and makes your certificate look more professional when you share it.
- **Notification preferences.** Choose how and when you'd like to hear from AI Academy. You can enable weekly progress reminders, course update notifications, and community digest emails — or turn them off entirely.
- **Learning goals.** Set a weekly learning goal in your dashboard. Options range from "Casual" (1 lesson per week) to "Intensive" (5+ lessons per week). AI Academy will send you gentle reminders to help you stay on track with the target you set.

<Note>
  A free AI Academy account gives you full access to all introductory content, including the first lesson of every course and the complete Beginner Path preview. To unlock all courses, projects, and certificates, upgrade to a Pro account at any time from your dashboard.
</Note>

<Tip>
  **Not sure which learning path to pick?** If you're completely new to AI, choose the [Beginner Path](/paths/beginner). It's the most popular starting point on the platform and covers everything you need to know before moving on to more specialised topics. You can always switch paths or add more paths to your dashboard as your confidence grows.
</Tip>

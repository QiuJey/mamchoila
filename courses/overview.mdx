## How Courses Are Structured

Every course at AI Academy follows the same learner-friendly format so you always know what to expect.

**Video Lessons** — Each module starts with a short, engaging video that introduces the key ideas. Lessons are designed to be watched in one sitting — typically 5 to 15 minutes each.

**Reading Materials** — After each video, you'll find a written summary with additional context, definitions, and real-world examples to reinforce what you just learned.

**Quizzes** — Short knowledge checks at the end of each module help you confirm you've understood the material before moving on. You can retake them as many times as you need.

**Final Project** — Each course ends with a hands-on final project that brings everything together. Completing it earns you a shareable certificate of completion.

## Choosing the Right Course

Not sure where to begin? Think about what you want to accomplish — building general knowledge, learning to use AI tools, or getting better results from AI — and pick the course that matches your goal. You can take courses in any order, and many learners end up working through all of them.

<Tip>
  If you're completely new to AI, start with **AI Fundamentals**. It gives you the vocabulary and conceptual grounding that makes every other course easier to understand.
</Tip>

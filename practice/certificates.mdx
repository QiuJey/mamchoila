Certificates are AI Academy's way of recognising the work you've put in. When you complete a course — including all quizzes and a final project — you earn a verified certificate that you can share with employers, add to your professional profiles, or simply keep as a record of your progress. Each certificate is tied to a unique verification code, so anyone you share it with can confirm it's genuine.

## Requirements to Earn a Certificate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete all course modules">
    Work through every lesson in every module of the course. All content must be marked complete before you can move to the assessment stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pass all module quizzes">
    Each module quiz must be passed with a score of 70% or higher. You can retake quizzes as many times as you need until you meet the threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and pass the final project">
    Complete the course's required project and submit it for review. Your submission will be assessed against the project brief, and you'll receive written feedback. You must pass to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request your certificate from your dashboard">
    Once all requirements are met, go to the **Certificates** section of your dashboard and click **Request Certificate**. Your certificate will be generated and ready to download within a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's on Your Certificate

Every AI Academy certificate includes the following details:

- **Your full name** as entered in your learner profile
- **Course name** confirming which course or programme you completed
- **Completion date** showing when you fulfilled all requirements
- **Unique verification code** that anyone can use to confirm the certificate is authentic

## How to Share Your Certificate

Once your certificate is issued, you have several ways to put it to work:

- **Download as PDF** — Save a high-quality PDF to your device, ready to attach to job applications or email to a contact.
- **Share a verification link** — Copy a unique public link that anyone can open in a browser to view and verify your certificate instantly, no login required.
- **Add to your LinkedIn profile** — Display your certificate directly on your LinkedIn profile under the **Licences & Certifications** section to let your network know about your new skills.

<Tip>
  Adding your certificate to LinkedIn takes less than two minutes. From your certificate page in the dashboard, click **Add to LinkedIn**. AI Academy will pre-fill the issuer name, credential ID, and issue date — just confirm and save. Your certificate will appear on your profile right away.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Certificate verification is **permanent and publicly accessible**. Your verification link will remain active indefinitely, so you can share it now or years from now and it will always display your certificate details correctly.
</Note>

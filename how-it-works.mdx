AI Academy is structured to take you from curious beginner to confident AI user through a clear, step-by-step learning journey. Every element of the platform — from the way courses are sequenced to how projects are graded — is designed to keep you moving forward without feeling overwhelmed. Here's exactly what to expect from the moment you sign up.

## Your Learning Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up for a free AI Academy account using your email address. Your account tracks your progress across every course and learning path, so you can pick up exactly where you left off on any device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Learning Path">
    A learning path is a curated sequence of courses built around a specific goal — such as understanding AI fundamentals or mastering prompt engineering. Browse the available paths and select the one that matches where you are and where you want to go. If you're new to AI, start with the [Beginner Path](/paths/beginner).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take Courses">
    Each course inside a learning path is broken into short, focused lessons. Watch video explanations, read supporting material, and test your understanding with quizzes after each lesson. Lessons are designed to be completed in 10–15 minutes so you can learn in the gaps of a busy day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Projects">
    At the end of each course, you'll apply what you've learned through a hands-on project. Projects are reviewed against clear criteria, and you receive structured feedback to help you improve. Completing projects is what turns knowledge into real skill.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Your Certificate">
    Once you complete all the courses and projects in a learning path, you earn a shareable certificate. Your certificate includes your name, the learning path title, and a verification link — making it easy to share on LinkedIn or attach to a job application.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Learning Format

AI Academy uses a blend of formats to keep learning engaging and effective.

- **Video lessons.** Each concept is taught through clear, concise video lessons with on-screen examples and visuals. Videos are captioned and can be watched at 0.75×, 1×, or 1.5× speed.
- **Quizzes.** Short quizzes at the end of each lesson check your understanding and reinforce key ideas. You can retake quizzes as many times as you need.
- **Hands-on projects.** Every course ends with a practical project that challenges you to use what you've learned in a realistic scenario.
- **Reading materials.** Each lesson includes optional reading materials — summaries, glossaries, and further resources — for learners who want to go deeper.

## Self-Paced Learning and Progress Tracking

AI Academy is fully self-paced. There are no enrolment windows, no live sessions to attend, and no deadlines to meet. You set the schedule that works for your life.

Your dashboard gives you a clear view of your progress at all times. You can see which lessons you've completed, which projects are outstanding, and how far you are from earning your next certificate. If you ever step away for a while, your progress is saved automatically and you can return to exactly where you left off.

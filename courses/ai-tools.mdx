The best way to understand AI is to use it — and right now, there are more powerful, accessible AI tools available than ever before. This course gives you a practical, guided introduction to the AI tools that are already changing how people work, create, and communicate. You'll get hands-on experience with each one, learn when to use which tool, and finish the course with the confidence to bring AI into your everyday life or workflow.

## What You'll Learn

Five focused modules take you from understanding the landscape to putting everything together in your own workflow.

<Accordion title="Module 1: Navigating AI Chatbots">
  Get started with the most widely used AI tools in the world. You'll set up accounts, run your first conversations, and learn how to ask questions and give instructions in ways that get genuinely useful responses. This module also covers understanding the strengths and limitations of different chatbot tools so you can choose the right one for each task.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Module 2: AI for Writing & Editing">
  Explore how AI writing assistants can help you write faster and better. You'll practice using AI to draft emails, summarize articles, rewrite for tone and clarity, and check your work — all while staying in control of your own voice and ideas. Learn what to delegate to AI and where human judgment still wins.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Module 3: AI for Images & Visuals">
  Unlock your creative potential with AI image generation tools. You'll learn how to write descriptive prompts that produce the images you actually have in mind, how to iterate and refine results, and how to use generated visuals responsibly. By the end of the module, you'll have created your first AI-generated image from scratch.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Module 4: AI for Productivity & Automation">
  Discover how AI tools can take repetitive, time-consuming tasks off your plate. This module covers AI transcription and meeting summaries, document analysis, task management integrations, and simple automation — practical applications you can start using right away, no coding required.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Module 5: Putting It All Together">
  Build a personal AI toolkit tailored to your goals. You'll map out the tools that fit your life and work, practice moving between them fluidly, and complete your final project: a short showcase of how you used at least three different AI tools to accomplish a real task or creative goal.
</Accordion>

## By the End of This Course

After completing AI Tools, you will be able to:

- Use AI chatbots like ChatGPT and Claude effectively for a range of tasks
- Generate images from text descriptions using DALL-E or Midjourney
- Integrate AI writing assistants into your editing and communication workflow
- Identify and use AI productivity tools that match your work style
- Evaluate new AI tools as they emerge and decide when to adopt them
- Combine multiple AI tools to tackle complex, real-world tasks

## How to Participate

Getting started in the forums takes less than a minute. Follow these steps to join the conversation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [aiacademy.com](https://aiacademy.com) or log in if you already have an account. Your forum profile is created automatically alongside your student account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Community tab">
    From your student dashboard, click **Community** in the top navigation bar. This takes you directly to the forum home page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a forum section">
    Browse the list of forum sections and select the one that best matches what you want to discuss or ask about. Each section has a brief description to help you choose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask a question or share your work">
    Click **New Post**, add a clear title, and write your message. Include as much context as you can — the more detail you provide, the faster and more helpful the responses will be.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engage with replies">
    Check back on your posts regularly and respond to anyone who replies. Mark the most helpful answer as the **Accepted Answer** so future readers can find it quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Community Guidelines

The forums thrive because members treat each other with respect. Keep these principles in mind whenever you post or reply.

- **Be respectful.** Critique ideas, not people. Everyone here is learning, and a little encouragement goes a long way.
- **Stay on topic.** Post in the section that fits your message and keep threads focused so they stay useful for others searching later.
- **No spam or self-promotion.** Sharing genuinely helpful resources is welcome; advertising products or services for personal gain is not.
- **Help beginners.** If you see a question you can answer — even a simple one — take a moment to respond. You were a beginner once too.

<Tip>
  The **Project Showcase** section is one of the most rewarding places on the forums. Share your completed course projects, personal experiments, or any AI work you're proud of — even if it's rough around the edges. The community loves seeing real work, and the feedback you receive will accelerate your growth faster than going it alone.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The AI Academy forums are monitored by a team of trained community moderators who ensure discussions stay safe and constructive. Most posts receive a community reply within a few hours during peak times. For urgent course or account issues, reach out directly through the [support page](/support/troubleshooting) for a faster response.
</Note>

Self-paced learning lets you study on your own schedule, but some questions are best answered in real time. AI Academy's live sessions give you direct access to instructors, industry experts, and a room full of fellow learners — so you can ask follow-up questions, work through problems together, and pick up insights that no pre-recorded video can replicate. Live sessions are included as part of your AI Academy membership and are designed to fit into a busy schedule.

## Session Types

AI Academy runs four types of live sessions throughout the month, each designed to meet a different learning need.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Weekly Q&A" icon="microphone">
    Bring your questions to an open, instructor-led call every week. No agenda, no pressure — just show up with what's on your mind and get clear, direct answers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live Workshops" icon="chalkboard-user">
    Roll up your sleeves for guided, hands-on practice sessions. Workshops follow a structured format where an instructor walks you through a real AI task step by step as you build along.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Expert Guest Talks" icon="star">
    Hear directly from AI practitioners, researchers, and hiring managers working in the field today. Guest talks cover real-world applications, career paths, and trends you won't find in a textbook.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Course Office Hours" icon="clock">
    Need help with a specific course? Office hours are small-group sessions focused on one course at a time, giving you dedicated support from the instructor who built the material.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Join a Live Session

Registering for and joining a session is straightforward. Follow these steps to secure your spot.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Head to [aiacademy.com](https://aiacademy.com) and sign in. Live sessions are available to all active members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Events in the menu">
    From your dashboard, click **Events** in the main navigation. You'll see a calendar view alongside an upcoming sessions list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse upcoming sessions">
    Filter by session type, course, or date to find sessions that match your current interests or the course you're working through.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Register">
    Open any session and click the **Register** button. Your spot is confirmed instantly and the session is added to your personal events calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join at the scheduled time">
    At the session start time, use the join link sent to your registered email address or click **Join Now** directly from the Events page on your dashboard. Join a few minutes early to check your audio and video.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Missed a Session? Watch the Recording

Life gets busy — that's exactly why every AI Academy live session is recorded and made available after it ends. You can find recordings in the **Events** section of your dashboard under the **Past Sessions** tab, usually within a few hours of the session ending. Recordings include the full Q&A, so you won't miss the insights that came up on the call.

<Info>
  Session recordings are stored in your account for the duration of your active membership. Download or review them any time, as often as you need.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Get more out of Q&A sessions by preparing your questions in advance. Before joining, spend five minutes jotting down the specific concept you're stuck on, what you've already tried, and what outcome you were expecting. Focused questions lead to much more useful answers — and instructors love them.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Weekly Q&A** sessions run every Wednesday at 6 PM UTC. **Live Workshops** are held once a month, typically on the first Saturday of the month. **Expert Guest Talks** and **Course Office Hours** are scheduled on a rolling basis — check the Events page regularly or enable email notifications to stay informed about new sessions as they're added.
</Note>

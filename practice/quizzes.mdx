Quizzes are one of the most effective ways to turn passive learning into active recall. At AI Academy, quizzes appear throughout every course — not to trip you up, but to help you spot what you've understood, flag anything that needs another look, and build confidence as you progress. The more you engage with quizzes, the more the material sticks.

## Quiz Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Knowledge Checks" icon="circle-check">
    Short quizzes that appear after individual lessons. Knowledge checks confirm you've grasped the key idea before moving on, keeping your understanding sharp as you go.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Module Quizzes" icon="clipboard-list">
    Longer quizzes at the end of each module that test everything covered in that section. Passing a module quiz is required before you can progress to the next module.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Practice Exams" icon="file-pen">
    Full-length reviews that cover an entire course. Practice exams are great for consolidating your knowledge before you tackle a final project or request a certificate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flashcard Quizzes" icon="layer-group">
    Bite-sized vocabulary and concept reviews delivered as interactive flashcards. Use them at any time to reinforce key terms and definitions from the course.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How Quizzes Work

AI Academy quizzes are designed to be low-stress and genuinely helpful. Here's what you can expect every time you take one:

- **Multiple choice and true/false questions** — Questions are clear and focused on the core ideas from your lessons. There are no trick questions or ambiguous wording.
- **Instant feedback with explanations** — As soon as you answer, you'll see whether you were right or wrong along with a short explanation of why. This turns every question into a mini learning moment.
- **Retake as many times as you need** — There's no penalty for retaking a quiz. If you don't score as well as you'd like the first time, review the relevant lesson and try again.
- **Progress tracked in your dashboard** — Every quiz result is saved automatically. Your dashboard shows your scores over time so you can see how your understanding is developing.

<Tip>
  Even when you get a question right, take a moment to read the explanation. Correct answers sometimes include extra context or nuance that adds to your understanding — and it only takes a few seconds.
</Tip>

## How Scores Are Recorded

Your quiz scores are saved to your learner profile as soon as you submit. You can review your full quiz history at any time from the **Progress** section of your dashboard. Scores are used to track module completion and determine whether you're ready to move forward in the course.

If you retake a quiz, your most recent score replaces the previous one in your completion record, though your full history remains visible for your own reference. Strong quiz performance across a course also contributes to your eligibility to submit a final project and earn a certificate.

<Note>
  Module quizzes have a **minimum passing score of 70%**. You must meet this threshold to mark the module as complete and unlock the next one. If you don't pass on your first attempt, review the module lessons and retake the quiz — there's no limit on attempts.
</Note>

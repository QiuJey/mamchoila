This path is designed for professionals, managers, entrepreneurs, and business owners who want to use AI to get a competitive edge at work. You don't need a technical background or programming experience — this path focuses entirely on practical application. By the end, you'll know how to identify opportunities for AI in your organization, choose the right tools, and use them to save time, cut costs, and create better outcomes.

## Why AI Matters for Business

AI is no longer a technology reserved for large tech companies with dedicated engineering teams. It's available to every business, every department, and every individual contributor — right now, through tools that require no coding and minimal training. Organizations that adopt AI effectively are moving faster, serving customers better, and making smarter decisions.

The question is no longer *whether* to use AI at work. It's *how* to use it well. This path gives you the knowledge and skills to answer that question confidently.

## Your Learning Roadmap

This path builds from understanding to application. You'll start with a solid conceptual foundation, then move into tools and techniques you can use at work immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Week 1–4: AI Fundamentals">
    Build the mental model you need to make smart decisions about AI. You'll learn how AI systems work, what they can and cannot do, where they're already being used in business, and what to watch out for. This foundation ensures you can evaluate AI tools critically and communicate about them clearly with colleagues, leadership, and vendors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 5–6: AI Tools">
    Get hands-on with the AI tools most relevant to business use cases. You'll explore platforms for writing, research, automation, customer communication, and data summarization. You'll leave this section with a practical understanding of which tools fit which business problems — and how to get started with them quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 7: Prompt Engineering">
    The quality of what you get from AI depends almost entirely on how you ask for it. This course teaches you the prompting techniques that get better, more consistent results — whether you're drafting a proposal, analyzing a document, or automating a workflow. One week here will permanently improve how you use every AI tool you encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply AI in a Real Business Project">
    Bring everything together by applying AI to a real challenge in your own work or business. Identify a problem, choose the right tools, build a solution using what you've learned, and document your results. This is your capstone — and it produces something genuinely useful, not just an academic exercise. Submit your project to earn your certificate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

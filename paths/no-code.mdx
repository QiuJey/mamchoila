This path is built for people who want to harness the full power of AI without ever touching a line of code. If you're a writer, marketer, entrepreneur, educator, designer, or anyone who wants to work smarter — not harder — this path shows you exactly how to do that. You don't need a technical background, a computer science degree, or any programming experience whatsoever. You just need curiosity and a willingness to experiment.

## Why No-Code AI Matters

Modern AI tools have fundamentally changed who can build and automate things. A few years ago, using AI required deep technical expertise. Today, the most powerful AI systems in the world respond to plain English. You can generate images, draft documents, analyze data, automate workflows, and run entire business processes — all by describing what you want in your own words.

The bottleneck is no longer technical skill. It's knowing *how* to communicate with AI effectively. That's exactly what this path teaches you.

## Your Learning Roadmap

This path starts with the tools themselves so you can see what's possible right away, then dives deep into the skill that unlocks everything: prompt engineering.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Week 1–2: AI Tools">
    Dive straight into the tools that are changing how people work and create. You'll get hands-on experience with AI chatbots, image generators, writing assistants, and automation platforms — learning what each tool does best and when to use it. By the end of week two, you'll have a go-to toolkit ready for real tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 3–5: Prompt Engineering">
    Now sharpen the skill that makes every AI tool dramatically more useful. You'll learn how to structure your requests, provide context, iterate on outputs, and develop a personal library of prompts tailored to your specific work. This is where good AI users become great ones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete a No-Code AI Project">
    Apply what you've learned by completing a no-code AI project of your choosing. This could be building an automated content workflow, creating a set of AI-generated visuals, or designing a prompt system that solves a real problem in your life or work. Your project demonstrates your skills to yourself — and to others.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Your Certificate">
    Submit your project and earn your **AI Academy Certificate of Completion**. Share it on LinkedIn, add it to your resume, or use it to show clients and employers that you know how to work with AI — no code required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

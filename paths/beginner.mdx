## Your Learning Roadmap

Follow these steps in order to get the most out of this path. Each stage builds on the last, so you'll always feel prepared before moving forward.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Week 1–4: AI Fundamentals">
    Kick off your journey by understanding what AI actually is, how it works, and why it matters. You'll explore the history of AI, learn the difference between narrow and general AI, and discover how machine learning models are trained. By the end of this course, you'll be able to speak confidently about AI concepts and separate fact from hype.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 5–7: Prompt Engineering">
    Now that you understand AI, learn how to communicate with it effectively. Prompt engineering teaches you how to write instructions that get useful, accurate, and creative results from AI systems. You'll practice with real examples and develop a personal toolkit of prompting strategies you can reuse across any AI tool.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 8–9: AI Tools">
    Put your knowledge into practice by exploring the most powerful AI tools available today. You'll learn how to use AI writing assistants, image generators, productivity tools, and automation platforms — all without writing a single line of code. These are skills you can apply immediately in your work and daily life.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Final Project and Earn Your Certificate">
    Bring everything together in a final project that demonstrates what you've learned. You'll apply AI tools and prompting techniques to solve a real-world problem of your choosing. Submit your project to earn your **AI Academy Completion Certificate** — a credential you can share on your resume and LinkedIn profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You'll Be Able to Do After Completion

By the time you finish this path, you will be able to:

- Explain what AI is, how it learns, and where it's being used in the real world
- Write effective prompts that produce high-quality results from any AI system
- Choose the right AI tool for a given task and use it confidently
- Automate repetitive tasks and boost your personal productivity with AI
- Evaluate AI outputs critically and understand their limitations
- Start exploring more advanced AI topics with a strong conceptual foundation

<Note>
  This path is completely self-paced. The week estimates are guidelines — not deadlines. Some learners finish in five weeks; others take three months. Move as fast or as slowly as you need to, and revisit any lesson at any time.
</Note>

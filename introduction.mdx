AI Academy is an online learning platform built for people who want to understand and work with artificial intelligence — even if they've never written a line of code or taken a computer science class. Every course, lesson, and project is designed with the beginner in mind, giving you the knowledge, tools, and confidence to navigate the world of AI at your own pace.

## Why AI Academy

There are plenty of AI courses on the internet, but most are built for developers or researchers. AI Academy is different.

- **Beginner-friendly by design.** Every concept is introduced step by step, with plain-English explanations and visual examples that make complex ideas easy to grasp.
- **Self-paced learning.** There are no deadlines, no fixed schedules, and no pressure. Learn five minutes a day or five hours on a weekend — it's entirely up to you.
- **Recognized certificates.** When you complete a learning path, you earn a certificate you can share on LinkedIn, add to your CV, or show to your employer.
- **Supportive community.** You're not learning alone. Connect with fellow learners in the AI Academy community forum, ask questions, and share your progress.

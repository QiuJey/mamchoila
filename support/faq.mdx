Whether you're deciding whether to enroll or you've already started your first course, you probably have questions. This page collects the answers to the things learners ask most often — from prerequisites and pricing to certificates and technical requirements. If you don't find what you're looking for here, the community forums and support team are always ready to help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Do I need coding or math skills to learn AI here?">
  No prior coding or math experience is required to get started at AI Academy. Our beginner courses are designed to introduce you to AI concepts from the ground up, with plain-language explanations and visual examples before any technical content appears. As you progress to intermediate and advanced courses, you'll gradually build the Python and math skills you need — at a pace that makes sense.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are the courses free or paid?">
  AI Academy offers both free and paid content. A selection of introductory courses and lessons are available at no cost so you can explore the platform before committing. Full access to all courses, certificates, live sessions, and community features is included with an AI Academy membership. Check your account dashboard for current plan options and membership details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does each course take?">
  Course length varies depending on the topic and depth. Most beginner courses take between four and eight hours to complete, while intermediate and advanced courses typically range from ten to twenty hours. Each course page shows an estimated completion time, and because you learn at your own pace, there's no deadline to worry about.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I learn at my own pace?">
  Yes — all AI Academy courses are fully self-paced. You can start, pause, and resume any course whenever it suits your schedule. Your progress is saved automatically, so you can pick up right where you left off, whether that's the next day or the next month.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are the certificates recognized by employers?">
  AI Academy certificates are increasingly recognized by employers as a credible signal of practical AI skills. Each certificate specifies the skills and tools covered in the course, making it easy to reference in a resume or LinkedIn profile. While recognition varies by employer and industry, many of our graduates have successfully used their certificates to land roles or demonstrate upskilling to their current employers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What do I need to earn a certificate?">
  To earn a certificate for a course, you need to complete all lessons, pass the end-of-course assessment with a minimum score of 80%, and submit any required hands-on project for review. Once all requirements are met, your certificate is generated automatically and available to download from your profile within 24 hours.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I retake quizzes and projects?">
  Yes. You can retake any quiz as many times as you need to. For projects, you may resubmit up to three times after receiving feedback. The goal is to make sure you genuinely understand the material — not just to pass on the first try. Your highest passing score is the one recorded on your transcript.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
  Go to the [login page](https://aiacademy.com/login) and click **Forgot your password?** Enter the email address associated with your account and check your inbox for a reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes, so use it promptly. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. Still having trouble? Visit the [troubleshooting page](/support/troubleshooting) for further steps.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What AI tools will I use in the courses?">
  The tools you encounter depend on the courses you take. Across the curriculum you'll work with widely used tools and platforms including Python, Jupyter Notebooks, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Hugging Face, OpenAI APIs, and various data visualization libraries. Each course lists the tools it covers on its overview page so you know what to expect before you begin.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a mobile app?">
  Yes. The AI Academy mobile app is available for iOS and Android. You can watch lessons, read course materials, and track your progress directly from your phone or tablet. Hands-on coding exercises are best completed on a desktop or laptop, but the app is great for keeping your learning moving on the go.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I join the community forums?">
  Your forum access is set up automatically when you create an AI Academy account — there's no separate sign-up needed. From your dashboard, click **Community** in the navigation menu to go directly to the forums. Read through the community guidelines before posting your first message to make sure your experience (and everyone else's) stays positive.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I get stuck on a lesson?">
  Getting stuck is a normal part of learning — here's how to get unstuck quickly. First, re-read the lesson notes or rewatch the video, pausing to take notes. If that doesn't help, search the **Course Help & Questions** section of the [community forums](/community/forums) — there's a good chance someone else asked the same thing. You can also post your specific question there, or bring it to the next [Weekly Q&A live session](/community/live-sessions) for a direct answer from an instructor.
</Accordion>

<Note>
  For technical problems like login errors, video playback issues, or missing certificates, head to the [Troubleshooting page](/support/troubleshooting) for step-by-step fixes organised by issue type.
</Note>

Most technical hiccups on AI Academy have a quick fix. This page walks you through the most common issues learners encounter — organised by category — so you can get back to learning as fast as possible. Work through the relevant steps in order, and if nothing here resolves your problem, the support team is ready to help directly.

## Account & Login Issues

<Accordion title="Can't log in or forgot your password">
  If you can't access your account, follow these steps in order:

  1. Double-check that you're entering the email address you used when you signed up. Try copying and pasting rather than typing.
  2. Click **Forgot your password?** on the [login page](https://aiacademy.com/login) and enter your email address to receive a reset link.
  3. Check your inbox for an email from **no-reply@aiacademy.com**. If it isn't there within two minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
  4. Click the reset link in the email and create a new password. The link is valid for 30 minutes — if it expires, repeat the process.
  5. Once your password is reset, clear your browser cache and try logging in again.

  If you signed up using **Google** or **Apple** single sign-on, make sure you're clicking the correct sign-in button rather than entering a password manually.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Email not verified — resend the verification email">
  When you first create an account, AI Academy sends a verification email to confirm your address. If you skipped this step or the email got lost, here's how to resend it:

  1. Log in to your account (unverified accounts can still log in).
  2. You'll see a yellow banner at the top of your dashboard prompting you to verify your email. Click **Resend Verification Email**.
  3. Check your inbox and spam folder for an email from **no-reply@aiacademy.com**.
  4. Click the link in the email to complete verification. The link expires after 24 hours.

  If the email still doesn't arrive, make sure your email address on your profile is correct. Go to **Settings → Account → Email Address** to check and update it, then request a new verification email.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Account locked — what to do">
  Accounts are temporarily locked after five consecutive failed login attempts as a security measure. The lock lifts automatically after 30 minutes. To resolve it sooner:

  1. Use the **Forgot your password?** link on the login page to reset your password immediately — this also unlocks the account.
  2. If you believe your account was locked due to suspicious activity you didn't initiate, contact support right away so the team can review your account security.

  Once you're back in, consider enabling **Two-Factor Authentication** under **Settings → Security** to protect your account going forward.
</Accordion>

## Course & Progress Issues

<Accordion title="Progress not saving">
  If your course progress isn't being recorded, try these steps:

  1. **Refresh the page** — sometimes a simple reload is all it takes to sync your progress to the server.
  2. **Check your internet connection** — progress saves in real time and requires a stable connection. Switch networks or reconnect if you're on an unstable signal.
  3. **Try a different browser** — Chrome, Firefox, and Edge all work well with AI Academy. If you're on Safari, make sure it's up to date.
  4. **Clear your browser cache** — go to your browser settings, clear cached files and cookies for aiacademy.com, then reload the course.
  5. **Disable browser extensions** — ad blockers and script blockers can sometimes interfere with progress tracking. Try disabling them temporarily.

  If the issue continues after trying all of these steps, contact support with your course name and the lesson where progress stopped — this helps the team investigate quickly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Video not playing">
  Follow this checklist if a course video won't load or play:

  1. **Refresh the page** and wait a few seconds before pressing play again.
  2. **Check your internet speed** — streaming video comfortably requires at least 5 Mbps. Run a quick speed test if you're unsure.
  3. **Lower the video quality** — click the settings icon on the video player and select a lower resolution (360p or 480p) to reduce buffering.
  4. **Try a different browser** — if the video fails in one browser, test it in another to rule out a browser-specific issue.
  5. **Disable VPN or proxy** — some VPN configurations block video content. Temporarily turn off your VPN and try again.
  6. **Clear browser cache and cookies** for aiacademy.com, then reload the page.
  7. **Check for browser extensions** that might block media playback, particularly content blockers or privacy extensions.

  If none of these steps work, the issue may be on our end. Check the [AI Academy status page](https://status.aiacademy.com) to see if there's an active incident.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Quiz answers not submitting">
  If you're unable to submit a quiz or receive an error after clicking submit, try the following:

  1. Make sure you've answered every question — the submit button is disabled until all questions have a selected answer.
  2. Refresh the page. If your answers are still selected after the reload, try submitting again.
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies, reload the quiz, and re-enter your answers.
  4. Disable any browser extensions that might interfere with form submissions, such as script blockers or ad blockers.
  5. Try submitting from a different browser or device.

  Avoid using the browser's back button during a quiz — this can cause the session to time out and prevent submission.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Certificate not appearing after course completion">
  Certificates are usually generated within a few minutes of completing all course requirements. If yours hasn't appeared, check the following:

  1. Confirm you've completed **every lesson** in the course — check the course outline for any lessons still marked as incomplete.
  2. Confirm you've passed the **end-of-course assessment** with a score of at least 80%. You can review your score in the course's **Results** tab.
  3. If a hands-on **project submission** is required, make sure it has been reviewed and approved. Project reviews can take up to 48 hours.
  4. Navigate to **Profile → Certificates** to check whether the certificate is there but not visible on the course page.
  5. If all requirements are met and more than 24 hours have passed, contact support with your course name and account email so the team can issue your certificate manually.
</Accordion>

## Billing & Subscription Issues

<Accordion title="Charge not recognised on your statement">
  If you see a charge from AI Academy that you don't recognise, here's how to identify it:

  1. Check whether anyone else with access to your payment method may have signed up for or renewed an AI Academy subscription.
  2. Log in to your account and go to **Settings → Billing → Payment History** to view a full list of charges associated with your account.
  3. Look for a charge dated on or around when the statement charge appeared — subscription renewals happen automatically on your billing cycle date.

  If the charge still seems incorrect after reviewing your billing history, contact support with your account email and the charge amount so the team can investigate and resolve it promptly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to cancel your subscription">
  You can cancel your AI Academy subscription at any time directly from your account settings — no need to contact support.

  1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Subscription**.
  2. Click **Cancel Subscription** and follow the on-screen prompts.
  3. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged again after that.

  After cancelling, your course progress and certificates are preserved. If you reactivate your membership later, everything will be right where you left it.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Refund policy">
  AI Academy offers a **7-day money-back guarantee** on new monthly and annual subscriptions. If you're not satisfied within the first seven days of a new subscription, contact support at **support@aiacademy.com** to request a full refund.

  Refunds are not available for subscription renewals or for partial periods after the 7-day window has passed. Individual course purchases (if applicable) follow the same 7-day policy from the date of purchase.

  Approved refunds are processed within five to ten business days, depending on your payment provider.
</Accordion>

<Note>
  If none of the steps on this page resolve your issue, reach out to the AI Academy support team directly at **support@aiacademy.com**. Include your account email address, a brief description of the problem, and any error messages you've seen — this helps the team diagnose and fix your issue as quickly as possible. The support team typically responds within one business day.
</Note>
